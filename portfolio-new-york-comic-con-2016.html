    These posters were created for the final project of my Core Typography class. The assignment was to choose a previous large-scale event and create one main poster for the entirety of the event, and two smaller posters for select parts of the main event.

    I used a custom font called Avengers for titles, which fit the theme perfectly. My goal was to hint at the nature of graphic novels without overdoing it, which I believe I succeeded by limiting my color pallete, and the amount of typefaces I could use.
